How to make Guacamole

Home

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods! This quick and easy guacamole recipe is (almost) too good to be true.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl.
  3. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic.
  4. Season with lime juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
